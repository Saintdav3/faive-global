What I Offer

Five ways I can show up for your brand.

From a single campaign to an ongoing content partnership — here's how we can work together to create something your audience will actually care about.

01
Influencer Campaigns

Authentic content created and shared directly with my engaged beauty audience to increase brand awareness, drive product discovery, and generate real conversions.

  • Dedicated sponsored posts on TikTok and Instagram
  • Honest product reviews with educational context
  • Audience-aligned messaging and natural integration
  • Campaign reporting with views, engagement, and reach
02
UGC Content

High-quality user-generated content delivered for brand-owned use — ads, websites, email, organic social, and beyond. You own it. You use it anywhere.

  • Raw footage + edited final deliverables
  • Multiple variations for A/B testing
  • On-brand scripting and visual direction
  • Fast turnaround with revision rounds
03
Product Demonstrations

Clear, engaging product-focused videos that walk viewers through how your product works, why it works, and what real results look like — in a way that builds trust and confidence.

  • Before-and-after application walkthroughs
  • Ingredient and benefit breakdowns
  • Routine integration and how-to content
  • Skin type and concern-specific messaging
04
Storytelling Content

Creative concepts that weave your product naturally into relatable, real-life experiences — the kind of content people watch all the way through and share with their friends.

  • Day-in-my-life and routine-based formats
  • Narrative-led content with emotional resonance
  • Trend-aware hooks with authentic framing
  • Long-form and short-form variations
05
Short-Form Video Production

Vertical video content built specifically for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts — designed to stop the scroll, capture attention in the first 3 seconds, and drive action.

  • Native format optimised for each platform
  • Trend-informed creative direction
  • Hook-first scripting and editing
  • Paid ad-ready versions available
How It Works

From brief to published in four steps.

A straightforward process designed to keep things smooth on both ends — from the first conversation to final delivery.

01
Discovery Call

We connect to discuss your brand, goals, target audience, and the type of content that fits your campaign. No fluff, just clarity.

02
Brief & Proposal

I put together a tailored content proposal — format, timeline, deliverables, and pricing — based on what your brand actually needs.

03
Production

Content is scripted, filmed, and edited. You get a preview before anything goes live. Revisions are included in every package.

04
Delivery & Results

Content is delivered on the agreed date along with performance data — views, reach, engagement, and any other metrics we agreed to track.
